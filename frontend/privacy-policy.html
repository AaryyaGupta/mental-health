Privacy Policy

How Zephy handles, protects and respects your data & anonymity

1. Core Principles

Your emotional safety and dignity come first. We minimize personally identifiable data and avoid storing unnecessary information.

2. What We Collect

  • Auth identifiers (email or OAuth provider tokens)
  • Anonymous community posts & reactions
  • Poll participation (aggregated only)
  • Fit Check wellness metrics (local or anonymized)

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • No real names (unless contained in user text)
  • No precise location data
  • No biometric or medical records

4. AI Chat

Conversations may be processed transiently to generate responses. We do not intentionally profile or resell any content.

5. Data Retention

Non‑critical logs are periodically pruned. You may request deletion of account‑linked data where technically feasible.

6. Safety & Escalation

If harmful or self‑harm content is detected, we surface supportive guidance—not emergency intervention. Seek professional help when needed.

7. Changes

We may update the policy to improve clarity or compliance. Material changes will be highlighted in the Help Center.
